Yosan: the Budget Game

For my minor capstone project, I decided to continue a game that a group and I developed in a previous class. That game was called Yosan. Yosan is game with a fun and colorful environment where the player learns how to budget and make money . The player will be portrayed by a girl, named Sana who is the daughter of a shopkeeper. One day, Sana finds out her mother has left for a journey, leaving her with the family item shop. She is now forced to manage the shop without her mother by her side. In the beginning, their personal accountant and family friend Sunny, aids her in understanding the basics of commerce. She teaches Sana how to sell items as well as how to buy items to help restock and create revenue for their store. While getting the hang of managing the store, portals suddenly start appearing in their shop. Sana not only faces the responsibility for taking care of the shop, but she is also forced to make payments and fight monsters to make the portals disappear.

Pre-made assets were used an referenced. Game was developed in Unity using both C# and JavaScript.

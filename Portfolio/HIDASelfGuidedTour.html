Herberger Institute for Design and the Arts Self Guided Tour Map

This project is currently a work in progress. At the Arizona State University Herberger Institute for Design and the Arts, we hold tours for each specific school at specific times. Prospective students and their parents sometimes may not be able to visit at the assigned time or even visit the campus since they are out of state. This self guided tour map will help them get a feel of the Institute's facilities without the presents of a staff member. Some design considerations is to reduce the number or images to just the building. The map image and the layout was made by me in Adobe Illustrator.

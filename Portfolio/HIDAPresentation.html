Herberger Institute Presentation

While working in the Herberger Institute Recruitment and admissions team at Arizona State University, I not only speak to prospective students, but I am involved in designing some of our recruitment materials. One of the project that I have done was to redesign our presentation slides for our recruitment sessions. I followed the strict color standards of ASU and the Herberger Institute while trying to a convey current information for each school. The slides where made in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and imported to PowerPoint.
